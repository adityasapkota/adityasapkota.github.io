About Me

I am a Master of Science student in Computer Science at the University of North Dakota, where I work as a Graduate Research Assistant at the Center for Cyber Security Research under Dr. Prakash Ranganathan. My research focuses on applying machine learning and deep learning to cybersecurity challenges—including anomaly detection, federated learning for edge security, and AI-driven threat intelligence.

I bring over three years of industry experience as a Cloud Engineer at RapidCircle, where I architected and managed enterprise-scale Microsoft 365 and Azure environments across global clients. I am also an AI & Technology Intern at the ND Small Business Development Center, where I help small businesses adopt AI-powered tools and workflows.

I am passionate about the intersection of cloud infrastructure, AI/ML, and security—particularly building intelligent, automated systems that make organizations more resilient. Originally from Nepal, I am currently based in North Dakota.
