About

Computer Science graduate student at the University of North Dakota, researching cybersecurity at the Center for Cyber Security Research. My current focus: federated learning and anomaly detection for edge infrastructure. The practical question I keep returning to is how to build ML models that catch threats in real infrastructure, before they become incidents.

Before grad school, I spent three and a half years at RapidCircle as a Cloud Engineer, deploying and running Microsoft 365 and Azure environments for enterprise clients across Europe and Asia-Pacific. That work shapes how I think about research — I care about what actually holds up in production.

Alongside my graduate work, I help North Dakota small businesses at the ND SBDC figure out what AI can realistically do for their operations. Originally from Nepal. Based in Columbia, SC.
